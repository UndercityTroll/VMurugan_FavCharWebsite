Ancient Cave Paintings - 28,000 BC

Ancient Cave paintings often showed animals with multiple blurred limbs to represent movement. The age of these of many of the cave paintings have been varied from nearly 20,000 years old to 64,000 years old.

Shadow Puppets - 2000 BC

Shadow puppetry uses intracately cut 2d puppets that are moved by a puppeteer in front of a light source, thus casting a shadow on the screen for the audience to see. Originally from India and South East Asia in the 1,000 BC. Video example is a shadow puppet preformance of the Indian Epic Ramayana in Kerala, India.

Phenakistiscope - 1833

Used rapid substitution of images by rotating a disc to produce the illusion of motion. Invented in 1832 by Belgian researcher Joseph Plateau.

Katsudō Shashin - 1907

Earliest example of animation in Japan. It's origins and creator are unknown as it was only rediscovered in 2005. The film plays at 16 fps and shows a boy in a sailor suit writing "Katsudō Shashin" in kanji on a wall which translates to "moving picture".

Fantasmagorie - 1908

Fantasmagorie, created by Emile Cohl in 1908, is stated to be the first animation created using tradition methods where individual frames are drawn on paper and exposed on negative film. The animation depicts a stick figure morphing into various objects and indcludes live action portion where the animator's hand enters the film and interacts with the characters.

The Mechanical Doll - 1922

Produced by Fleischer Studios, where Max Fleischer developed and then patented a technique called rotoscoping where animation is based off of a live reference for more life-like movement. The Mechanical Doll began as an experiment rotoscoping images of Dave Fleischer preforming as a clown, which later evolved into the character, Koko the Clown.

Momotaro: Sacred Sailors - 1944

During World War 2, most countries used animation for propaganda purposes. Japan's Navy hired Mitsuyo Seo to create Momotaro: Sacred Sailors. The story is based off of a traditional Japanese folk tail, Momotaro, about a magical boy born out of a peach who teams up with a bear, dog, monkey, and pheasant to defeant monsters. The propoganda placed Momotaro as a general and the animals as soldiers fighting for Japan. Although meant to be a piece of propganda, Seo was inspired by Disney's Fantasia, and created the movie to inspire children and promote peace.

Astro Boy - 1963

Known in Japan by it's orignal name, Mighty Atom, Astro Boy is of the most influential anime series created as the first popular animated japanese cartoon. Written and directed by Osamu Tezuka, "Father of manga". It defined the anime style with large detailed eyes and spikey hair. It could also be classified as the first shonen series.

Dragon Ball - 1986

One of the most popular shonen series from Shonen Jump. It defined many key troupes and genres like tournament arc's and power escalation. Its iconic characters, fight scenes, and story lead it to be a global hit.

Spirited Away - 2001

Highest grossing anime movie, until Kimi no Nawa (Your Name) in 2016. Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, concidered to be the greatest anime directer from Studio Ghibli, with numerous other movies like "My Neighbor Totoro" and "Howl's Moving Castle". Spirited Away features brilliant animation and intricate storytelling, becoming the first and only japanese animation to win an Oscar for Best Animated Picture.

Full Metal Alchemist - 2003

The original adaptation of the hit manga series by the same name, Full Metal Alchemist or FMA, ran for only 1 year and split from the path of the source material as the manga hadn't been finished. The original anime adaptation was well recieved but was not a major defining piece of anime.

Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood - 2009

An full adaptation of the manga source material. It was critically acclaimed and became one of the most popular anime of all time being rated as the highest rated anime of all time according to MyAnimeList. Its complex story discussing power, morality, and the value of human life along with lovable characters and brilliant animation make it one of the unique anime of all time.
